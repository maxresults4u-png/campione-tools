📖 Signal Reference Guide

🎯 The Confluence Score (0-100)

Each signal adds points. More signals aligned = higher score. The score tells you setup quality, not when to trade.

85-100 🔥
Extreme — A++ setup
70-84 🟠
Strong — trade-worthy
50-69 🟡
Moderate — watch
0-49 ⚪
Weak — skip

📊 RSI (Relative Strength Index)

Measures how overbought/oversold the market is right now. Extremes suggest mean reversion.

RSI < 30 (Oversold)+20 pts · LONG · likely bounce
RSI 30-40 (Low)+12 pts · LONG (weak lean)
RSI 60-70 (High)+12 pts · SHORT (weak lean)
RSI > 70 (Overbought)+20 pts · SHORT · likely pullback

⚠️ In strong trends, RSI can stay extreme for hours. Always confirm with EMA.

📊 EMA Ribbon (9/21)

Measures trend direction and strength.

Price > EMA9 > EMA21+15 pts · LONG (bullish trend)
Price < EMA9 < EMA21+15 pts · SHORT (bearish trend)
Mixed alignment0 pts · no clear trend

🐋 Whale Activity (BTC/ETH only) — YOUR EDGE

Real-time data from your Bitcoin node mempool + Etherscan blocks. Large transactions = big players moving.

HIGH (3+ BTC / 5+ ETH whales)+25 pts · big activity NOW
MEDIUM (1-2 BTC / 2-4 ETH)+12 pts · some activity
LOW (quiet)0 pts

💡 Whales moving = SOMETHING is about to happen. Direction isn't known yet, just pressure. This is your unique edge.

📊 24-Hour Momentum (crypto)

How much has price moved in the last 24 hours. Strong momentum often continues.

> +3% (strong up)+15 pts · LONG
+1.5% to +3%+8 pts · LONG
-1.5% to -3%+8 pts · SHORT
< -3% (strong down)+15 pts · SHORT

📊 Position in 24h Range

Where the current price sits relative to today's high and low. Extremes often reverse.

Near 24h LOW (bottom 20%)+15 pts · LONG · support bounce
Mid-range (20-80%)0 pts · no edge
Near 24h HIGH (top 80%)+15 pts · SHORT · resistance rejection

📊 Volume Spike (indices only)

Current 5-min volume vs 20-period average. Big volume = conviction.

2x+ average+20 pts · strong conviction
1.5-2x average+10 pts · elevated
< 1.5x0 pts · normal

⏰ Session Filter

Liquidity quality. Trading during prime hours = tighter spreads, better fills, fewer fakeouts.

RTH Prime (9:30am-4pm EST)+15 pts · deep liquidity
US Crypto (9am-5pm EST)+10 pts · active
Off-hours0 pts · ⚠️ thin liquidity

⚠️ A 75+ score at 3am is SKEPTICAL — likely a low-liquidity fakeout.
